# Insight Hunter - Auto-CFO for Freelancers & Small Firms

AI-powered SaaS delivering CFO-level insights, forecasting, and reiports. Upload CSVs for instant dashboards, P&L projections, cash flow forecasts, and KPI alerts. Built for non-technical owners needing clarity fast.

Live MVP at v1.3.1 with Dashboard, Forecast, Reports, and Settings.

## Pricing Tiers & Features

### Overview by Tier

| Tier            | Target User                            | Core Outcome                                     |
|-----------------|----------------------------------------|--------------------------------------------------|
| Insight-Lite    | Solo freelancer / very small shop      | Basic clarity on cash flow and performance       |
| Standard        | Growing small business or micro-agency | Ongoing forecasting, alerts, and client-ready decks |
| Pro             | Fractional CFOs / small firms          | Multi-entity, white-label, and advanced automation |

### Insight-Lite (Free Basic)

**Goal**: Hook tier for quick value without friction.

**Features**:
- Manual CSV upload for bank exports and basic P&L files (1 company/profile).
- Simple dashboard: 12-month revenue, expenses, net, cash trends.
- Basic category breakdowns (top customers, vendors, expense buckets).
- One-click "Insight Report" PDF (lightweight).
- 3–6 month cash flow projection (trend-based).
- Monthly email summaries with key insights.
- **Limits**: Low transaction cap, no multi-entity/white-label/API/team seats.

### Standard ($$)

**Goal**: Default paid plan for real CFO value.

**Features** (builds on Lite):
- Automatic CSV ingestion (2–3 companies).
- Rich dashboard: 12–24 month trends, customer profitability, KPIs (MRR, AR days, runway).
- 12–18 month projections (P&L + cash flow) with basic scenarios.
- Branded PDF reports ("Board Pack", "Investor Snapshot").
- Weekly alerts for low runway, variances, KPI thresholds.
- 2–3 team seats.
- **Limits**: Higher caps, no white-label.

### Pro ($$$)

**Goal**: For fractional CFOs managing multiple clients.

**Features** (builds on Standard):
- Multi-tenant client management.
- White-label PDFs/emails.
- Scheduled monthly/quarterly reports per client.
- Advanced multi-scenario/driver-based forecasting.
- Priority integrations/API.
- Higher team seats with role-based access.
- Custom alerts per client.
- Priority support.
