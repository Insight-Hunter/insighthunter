FAQ
How does the BizForma 6-month Standard promotion work? +
Pay the BizForma formation fee and your account automatically upgrades to Standard for 6 months — no code needed. After 6 months, continue at $79/mo or move to Free. Your data is always preserved.
Can I mix plans and add-ons? +
Yes. Any plan can add any standalone service. A Free user could add Bookkeeping Basic at $19/mo. A Lite user could add Payroll at $39/mo. You only pay for what you use.
What's the difference between Pro Single and Pro Enterprise? +
Pro Single is for an owner managing their own business — one entity, full Pro features. Pro Enterprise is for fractional CFOs, accountants, and agencies managing multiple client businesses, with white-label branding and a multi-client portal.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes — no contracts, no cancellation fees. Cancel from Settings and your plan downgrades to Free at end of the billing period. Your data is never deleted.
Is there an annual discount? +
Yes — switch to annual billing and save 20%. Lite drops to $23/mo, Standard to $63/mo, Pro Single to $159/mo. Toggle above to see annual prices.
